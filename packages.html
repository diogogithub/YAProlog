:- Packages

YAP includes several external packages that work with YAP. Our very sincere thanks to the authors for allowing the inclusion of these packages. The packages are:

  • CHR, developed by Thom Fruewirth and Christian Holzbaur;

     

  • clp(Q,R), developed by Christian Holzbaur;

     

  • LogTalk: Open ource object-oriented extension to Prolog

     

  • Pillow 1.1 developed by the ciao group at UPM.

     

The CLP(BN) package is being developed at UFRJ and will soon be included in YAP.
